🇩🇪 Welcome!

This map shows worker-owned businesses in Germany — places where the employees collectively own and run the company.

🎯 What the colors mean

  • Worker Majority — 51%+ of employees are member-owners (true worker co-op)
  • Governance-based — Workers have strong decision-making power through the governance structure
  • Statute-based — Worker decision-making is guaranteed by the company's statutes
  • In Founding — In the process of being established
  • Unknown — Not yet classified
  • Remote-only — No public office, shown at city center

🔍 How to use it

  • Click any pin to see the business name, sector, and website
  • Filter by sector or governance type using the dropdowns above the map
  • Search by name or location using the search box
  • Zoom in — pins cluster when zoomed out and spread apart as you zoom in

➕ Know a business we're missing?

Click the "Add a business" button in the top right. Your submission will be reviewed and added to the map.
