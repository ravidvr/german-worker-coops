I built this map because I was curious: where are all the worker-owned businesses in Germany? Turns out, nobody had put them on a map before. So I scraped what was publicly available, geocoded every entry, and threw it on a Leaflet map with a submission form.

I like playing with AI, databases, maps, and anything that combines all three. This is one of those projects.
